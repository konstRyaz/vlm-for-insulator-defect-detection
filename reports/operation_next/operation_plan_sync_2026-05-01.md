## Tactical Interpretation

The Major's central hypothesis is supported: the bottleneck is not detector geometry or JSON stability, but crop-level semantic classification. The best improvement came from a discriminative visual backbone plus Qwen structured reporter, exactly as predicted.

The current champion is not a production-ready detector by itself. It still has notable `insulator_ok -> defect_flashover` confusion. However, it is now the strongest reproducible research result in the repo because it improves Stage 4 actual pipeline correctness by `+11/58` over the frozen Qwen baseline and also improves `defect_broken` recall.
